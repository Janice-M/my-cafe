KENYAN BOOKGEEKOUT CAFE



Kenyan BookGeekOut Cafe was founded in 2017 ; its sole aim was to provide a safe space for book lovers in Nairobi, where they can enjoy their favorite drink and read book of their choice in a serene and reader friendly environment. Since its inception, the cafe has hosted more than 7 comic cons and 16 book signings from both local and international authors .

For those who aspire to be bookworms but fear they are inadeqaute to visit us, fear not.This is your Hogwarts letter inviting you to come have a cup of your favorite beverage, fall in love with book and make friends who will become your tribe for life. Using our 'Sorting Hat' forms we will help you find the genre that quenches your reading thirst to satisfaction. You will also meet like minded people in our reading clubs who will motivate you to keep on reading. All this while you enjoy a cup of your favourite beverage and your favourite pastry.
